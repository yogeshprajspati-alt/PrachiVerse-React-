General Instructions

  1. The examination consists of 180 questions divided into four subjects.
  2. The total duration of the examination is 3 hours (180 minutes).
  3. Each question carries 4 marks for correct answer.
  4. 1 mark will be deducted for each incorrect answer.
  5. No marks will be awarded or deducted for unattempted questions.
  6. The maximum marks for the examination is 720.
  7. All questions are compulsory - there are no optional questions or internal choices.

Navigation Instructions

  • Use the question palette to navigate between questions.
  • Color codes in the palette:
    • Green - Answered
    • Red - Not Answered
    • Purple - Marked for Review
    • Gray - Not Visited
  • You can mark questions for review and come back to them later.
  • You can change your answers before final submission.
  • The timer will automatically submit the test when time expires.
  • A warning will appear when 10 minutes are remaining.

Important Reminders

  • Read each question carefully before answering.
  • Manage your time effectively across all subjects.
  • Review your answers before final submission.
  • Once you click "Final Submit", you cannot change your answers.
